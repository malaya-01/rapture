# Sanctuary Complete Bible

## Design philosophy

Sanctuary is not a city — it is a **promise**: humanity can survive, civilization can return, people can belong.

**Motto:** "We Survive Together."  
**Symbol:** The White Tree (roots=history, trunk=unity, branches=future)  
**Colors:** White · Silver · Emerald · Gold

---

## Evolution stages

| Stage | Population | Description |
|-------|------------|-------------|
| Camp | 100–500 | Tents, barricades, campfires |
| Settlement | 1K–5K | Wood homes, farms, watchtowers |
| Town | 10K–25K | Stone roads, schools, markets |
| City | 100K+ | Districts, academies, mana infrastructure |
| Great Sanctuary | 1M+ | Humanity's beacon |
