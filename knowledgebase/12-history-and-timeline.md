# History & Timeline

## Design philosophy

History feels: **ancient · layered · incomplete · contradictory**  
Not all records survived. Not all legends true. Not all myths false.

**Reader discovery:** The Rapture wasn't the beginning — it was the **latest cycle**.

---

| Age | Name | Notes |

| Outside | Inside |
|---------|--------|
| 3 years | 100 years |

Cassian experiences an entire lifetime — readers must feel that loss.

## Major mysteries

1. Who created the World Network?  
2. What caused the First Fracture?  
3. Why did Titans disappear?  
4. Why did dragons retreat?  
5. Why was Earth chosen?  
6. What is Limbo?  
7. What exists beyond dimensions?  
8. Is the Rapture natural or engineered?

## Ending timeline

Story ends with **recovery**, not victory alone: rebuilding, families, exploration, new generations, old heroes resting.

**Final image:** Elderly Cassian and Adrian (or Aria per late canon) on Sanctuary walls overlooking healed world — because they helped people save each other.
