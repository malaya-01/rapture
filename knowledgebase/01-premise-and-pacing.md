# Premise & Pacing

## Logline

The sky fractures. Dragons fall. Mana floods the Earth. In the ruins of everything familiar, Cassian Reed — an ordinary omega — and the people scattered around him must learn to survive, rebuild, and decide what kind of civilization deserves to inherit a world that has ended before.

## Core promise

Same **emotional engine** as *The Walking Dead*:

- Ordinary people in extraordinary collapse
- Found family under pressure
- Hope that costs something
- Deaths that matter
- Politics as dangerous as monsters

Molded through **Rapture** worldbuilding: mana, awakenings, dungeons, dragon domains, layered histories, Sanctuary.

## Themes

| Theme | Expression |
|-------|------------|
| Survival | Competence, scarcity, consequence |
| Found family | Highway Camp → Sanctuary |
| Leadership | Cassian refuses easy power |
| Legacy | Cycles of civilization |
| Identity | Omega misunderstood; Cassian's Limbo arc |
| Choice | Seventh Gate — civilizations choose their fate |
| Love | Slow burn; earned; not destiny |

## Audience & tone

**Adult (18+).** The apocalypse, combat, and survival are written with full consequence — blood, grief, brutality, and hazard are not softened. Romance includes affection and kissing when earned; explicit sex is not on page. See `23-content-maturity-rating.md`.

## Alpha / omega in this world

- Exists as **biology and culture**, not constant politics
- Alphas: often stronger physique / mana potential — not superior
- Betas: majority
- Omegas: rare, variable — Cassian subverts "fragile omega" trope through competence
- Romance uses secondary gender as **texture**, not fetish engine

## What readers should remember

**The people** — not power levels.

## Chapter 1 obligation

Destroy the ordinary world on schedule. End with the sky cracking.
