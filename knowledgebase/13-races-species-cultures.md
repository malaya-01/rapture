# Races, Species & Cultures

## Design philosophy

**No elves, dwarves, orcs, beastmen.** Every species evolved from: Mana · Dimensions · Ancient Ages · Rapture · Limbo.

Every intelligent species answers: Where from? How survive? What value? What fear? Why different from humans?

**No species exists solely to be fought.**

---

## Intelligent species tiers

1. Humans · 2. Dragonkind · 3. Ancient Species · 4. Dimensional · 5. Limbo-Born · 6. Cosmic Entities

---

## SPECIES 001 — HUMANS

Most adaptable; not strongest/smartest/longest-lived. Strengths: innovation, cooperation, creativity. Weaknesses: short lifespan, internal conflict.

Cultures: Sanctuary, Iron Dominion, Free Cities, Crimson Faith, Skyborne League, future nations.

---

## SPECIES 002 — DRAGONKIND

Ancient apex civilization. Territory-based **domains** (not kingdoms). Values: strength, wisdom, legacy. Weaknesses: pride, tradition.

Subspecies: Storm, Fire, Ice, Ocean, Void, Dream, Star, Frost, Earth (15–20 total target).

---

## SPECIES 003 — AETHERIANS

Humanoid, partially mana-composed; silver skin; glowing eyes; 500–800 year lifespan. Capital: **Archive Spires**. Weakness: emotionally detached.

---

## SPECIES 004 — THALASSARI

Deep-sea civilization; bioluminescent; city-states. Strengths: ocean knowledge, marine magic. Weakness: limited land ops.

---

## SPECIES 005 — SKYWALKERS

Floating island adapted; light build; gliding membranes. Values: exploration, horizon.

---

## SPECIES 006 — TERRAKIN

Stone-blooded; Age of Titans origin; crystal veins. Values: craftsmanship, memory.

---

## SPECIES 007 — ECHOBORN

Dimensional origin; star-fragment silhouettes; spatial awareness; identity instability.

---

## SPECIES 008 — LIMBO-BORN

Native to Limbo; varied forms; memory/possibility/identity. **Reveal slowly.** Many disciples originate here.

---

## SPECIES 009 — SYLVARI

World Tree descendants; plant features; life magic; conservation. Sensitive to corruption.

---

## SPECIES 010 — LUMINARI

Star-marked skin; cosmic travelers; navigation, storytelling.

---

## Hybrids

Rare — Human+Dragon, Human+Echoborn, etc. Every hybrid must **matter**.

---

## Cultural design rule

Every culture defines: history, values, government, military, economy, architecture, fashion, religion, cuisine, celebrations, burial, art, music.

---

## Major cultural conflicts

Humans vs Dragons (ownership of Earth) · Aetherians vs Iron Dominion (knowledge vs control) · Sylvari vs industrial expansion · Thalassari vs surface (ocean rights) · Echoborn vs reality stability

---

## Diplomacy roadmap

Early: mostly humans · Mid: human factions · Late: inter-species · Endgame: world-scale cooperation

---

## Targets

60–100+ intelligent civilizations total.

**Final rule:** World never feels human-centric. Humans important — not alone.
