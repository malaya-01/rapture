# Romance Roadmap

## Primary: Cassian Reed (omega) + Adrian Hale (alpha)

**Rule:** Journey first. Romance earned across ~1200 chapters — not the plot engine.

## Stages

| Stage | Chapters | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Strangers | 1–80 | Not yet met until ~81 |
| Friends | 81–500 | Cooperation, campfire talks, logistics trust |
| Best friends | 500–880 | Joint expeditions; Adrian nearly dies; Cassian saves him |
| Loss | 881–1080 | Cassian disappears (880); hidden return (1041) |
| Reunion | 1081–1140 | Identity reveal; grief resolution; feelings confronted — **do not rush** |
| Love | 1141–1175 | Partnership acknowledged |
| Partnership | 1176–1200 | Epilogue; life together; Adrian's peaceful death Vol 20 |

## Selene / Adrian (parallel — must read authentic)

- Genuine love Vol 1–12
- Jealousy **awareness** only from Ch ~116 (not accusations)
- First private breakdown Ch ~482 (Vol 8) — fear/exhaustion, not villainy
- Tragic collapse Vol 13–16 arc
- Readers must **mourn** this relationship

## Aria / Cassian (Limbo)

- Introduced Ch ~921; **no romance immediately**
- Dynamic: suspicion → cooperation → trust → decades → subtle mature romance
- First confession Ch ~964 (quiet, private)
- Farewell Ch ~1000: "Find me." / "I will."
- Reunion with Adrian arc must resolve separately — no love triangle melodrama

## Prose rules

- No attraction at Ch 81 meeting
- Subtext dominates early saga; **affection and kissing on page** once love is acknowledged (late saga) — see `23-content-maturity-rating.md`
- Secondary gender as texture, not fetish
- **Sexual intercourse:** implied or fade-to-black — **not** explicit pornographic prose
- Never reduce Cassian to omega stereotypes
- Selene/Adrian intimacy in early volumes reads **real** — warmth, touch, ordinary partnership (not explicit)

## End state

Elderly Cassian and Aria beneath Founder's Tree (Ch 1200). Adrian's story completes peacefully in Vol 20 — love transcends through grief and honesty, not replacement erasure.
