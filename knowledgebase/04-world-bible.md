# World Bible — The World After the Rapture

## SECTION 1 — THE RAPTURE

**Overview:** The event that ended modern civilization.

**Known names:** The Rapture · The Fracturing · Skyfall · The First Collapse · Day Zero

**Cause:** Unknown for most of the story.

### What happened (Day Zero)

On an ordinary day: sky fractured; massive **silver rifts** worldwide. Within hours: dragons emerged, monsters appeared, mana flooded Earth, physics changed, ecosystems mutated. Civilization collapsed.

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| First collapse period | ~18 months |
| Governments | Ceased functioning |
| Cities | Most fell |
| Casualties | Billions |

### Immediate effects

- **Mana saturation** — Earth infused with mana  
- **Spatial distortion** — geography changed; distances unreliable  
- **Biological mutation** — animals, plants, humans changed  
- **Dimensional overlap** — Earth partially merged with other realities  

---

## SECTION 2 — MANA

**Definition:** Naturally occurring cosmic energy. Existed before Rapture; Earth lacked concentration to utilize it.

**Laws:** Cannot be created · Cannot be destroyed · Can be transformed

### Mana density

| Level | Characteristics |
|-------|-----------------|
| Low | Pre-Rapture Earth; safe, stable |
| Medium | Most settlements; magical development possible |
| High | Monster territories, dungeons, ruins; dangerous |
| Catastrophic | Titans, dragon domains, rifts; reality unstable |

### Mana storms

Natural disasters from mana imbalance. Can: mutate creatures, alter terrain, **create dungeons**.

---

## SECTION 3 — AWAKENINGS

Some humans **awaken**; some do not. Not guaranteed. **No chosen one.**

**Growth via:** training · experience · understanding · creativity — **not levels**

### Affinity examples

Fire, Water, Earth, Wind, Lightning, Spatial, Healing, Shadow, Light, Beast, Rune, Growth — plus advanced (Gravity, Dream, Temporal) and rare (Void, Fate, Soul, Dimensional).

## SECTION 4 — EARTH AFTER THE FALL

Earth remains recognizable but transformed.

| Zone type | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Wild Zones** | Most territory; monster-controlled |
| **Human Territories** | Settlements → cities → kingdoms |
| **Dead Zones** | Rapture-scarred; almost nothing survives |
| **Rift Zones** | Reality unstable; extremely dangerous |
