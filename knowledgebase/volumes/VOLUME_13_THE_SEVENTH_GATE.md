## Segment beats (planning detail)

### A — Tremors (801–820)

- Golden Years prosperity shows hairline fractures: crop failures at wall edge, dreams of falling sky.
- Aetherian fragments (Vol 9) decode: Seventh Gate = emergency protocol when civilization exceeds reality.
- Selene advocates isolationism; Adrian mediates; Cassian studies instead of ruling.
- Young generation (Atlas, Leon, Lily) sense adults lying about danger.

### B — Convergence (821–845)

- Multi-faction summit collapses — Viktor demands control; Seraphine claims divine right; dragons withhold full history.
- Expedition roster: Cassian, Nora, Marcus, three Guard captains; Adrian **excluded** by Cassian's design.
- Gate interior previews: streets that loop, storage pockets that reject Cassian's affinity (wrongness).
- Selene sabotages supply line (minor); caught; public reprimand — sympathy maintained, trust damaged.

### C — Breach (846–870)

- Mega-saga set pieces (compressed, not padded): three linked catastrophes across regions.
- Skarax movement in north; Brood Mother pulse in east; Pale King memory theft in expedition rear guard.
- Velkarion fights to shield civilian corridors — dragon morality on display.
- Cassian stitches spatial tears at cost; each fix borrows from his future self (foreshadow Limbo time debt).

### D — Threshold (871–875)

- Gate presents choice: collapse outward (Rapture-scale repeat) or anchor inward (one steward).
- Cassian rejects "chosen one" framing — frames as **founder's obligation**, not destiny.
- Adrian confrontation: anger, acceptance; Rowan orders wall sealed behind expedition.
- Cassian leaves letters, not confessions; spatial keys passed to Marcus for Sanctuary vaults.

### E — Vanishing (876–880)

- Cassian crosses; interior = Limbo threshold (readers do not yet know name).
- Expedition survivors return with fragments; two die to wrong geometry.
- Public announcement: Founder lost; Adrian named acting First Guardian.
- Final image: Adrian at Founder's Tree, hand on bark Cassian planted — empty sky hums.

## POV & pacing notes

- **Mega-saga rule:** Every catastrophe chapter advances character OR relationship OR revelation — never spectacle alone.
- Rotate POV across factions during summit and cascade chapters.
- Selene POV minimum 6 chapters — reader must feel her fear, not villainy.
- No romance declaration; subtext peak at Ch 874 parting.
- Combat/chapters ratio ≤ 40%; grief and politics carry equal weight.

## Bridge to Volume 14

Sanctuary enters **Three Years** of official mourning. Adrian holds the city together while the world believes Cassian Reed is dead. The Gate hums unanswered. Limbo has already taken him — but the surface does not know that name yet.
