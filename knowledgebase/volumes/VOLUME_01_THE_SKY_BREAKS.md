## Volume metadata

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|

| **Title** | The Sky Breaks |
| **Chapters** | 1–30 |
| **Age** | 1 — Survival |
| **Macro arc** | The Last Ordinary Days (1–10) → opening of The First Month (11–30) |
| **Primary theme** | Loss |
| **Secondary theme** | Survival |
| **Purpose** | Destroy old world; introduce cast; scatter protagonists; establish Rapture rules; first dungeon sighting on horizon |

**Pacing contract:** Rapture begins **Chapter 1** — sky fractures by chapter end. No single POV for 30+ consecutive chapters. Civilization collapses Ch 2–4; monsters on page Ch 4–5. Cast paths cross **before Chapter 20** (indirectly — same events, not yet united). Monsters pressure; **people are the story**. No hero power fantasy; no rescue arc. Volume ends with the world unrecognizable and something impossible rising beyond the ruins.

**Location IDs:** Canonical codex IDs for chapter `locations:` frontmatter are in `04b-locations-encyclopedia.md` (Volume 1 table). When prose anchors a place, add the ID to seed and the encyclopedia — do not strip frontmatter locations from chapters.

## Pacing bridge to Volume 2

Volume 1 ends with destruction, scatter, and the first impossible silhouette. Volume 2 (*Alone*, Ch 31–70) opens on **complete isolation** — Cassian walks empty Ashford — and teaches readers how to survive: mana measured, awakenings frightening, dungeon rumors spreading until public manifestation. Do not reunite cast in Vol 2 until Highway Camp periphery (Ch 68–69). Adrian and Cassian remain strangers until ~Ch 81 (Vol 3).
