## Segment beats (planning detail)

### Act I — Stranger at the Gate (1041–1053)

- Cassian exits Gate weakened spiritually, not physically buffed — Limbo cost visible in pauses.
- Aria absent on surface; carries grief quietly.
- Wanderer's message in pocket — untranslated urgency.
- Cassian watches Adrian lead — pride and pain; chooses hidden return to test city without myth.
- Hired as logistical specialist; spatial talent disguised as rune assist tools.

### Act II — Echoes (1054–1067)

- Limbo disciple phrases appear in "Cade's" casual speech — Marcus and Lily notice.
- Nora's pattern recognition; Whisper's animal instinct vs Cassian's control.
- Atlas sees tactical decisions only Cassian taught in war games years ago.
- Near-miss: Founder's vault; Cassian opens inner seal; blames Lily's prototype.
- Adrian assigns proximity — dramatic irony; reader feels reunion delayed.

### Act III — Almost Known (1068–1080)

- Marcus holds knowledge — wants Cassian to choose reveal timing.
- Council celebrates Gate stabilization; "Cade" credited anonymously.
- Adrian dreams wrong stars again — wakes to "Cade" on wall schedule.
- Selene absent (exiled); her shadow lifted from reunion arc.
- Vol 17 ends **before** reveal — tension peak at Adrian's unconscious recognition.
