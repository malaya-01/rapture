# Volume 4 — The First Dungeon

## Volume metadata

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Number** | 4 |
| **Title** | The First Dungeon |
| **Chapters** | 121–180 |
| **Age** | 1 — Survival |
| **Macro arc** | Broken Roads / early Highway Camp transition |
| **Primary theme** | Discovery |
| **Secondary theme** | Trust |
| **Purpose** | Dungeon descent; ancient civilization; mana mastery seeds; friendships tested; **"We're not alone"** — map of connected worlds |

**Design rule:** Dungeon is not cave→boss→loot. It is the **bones of a lost civilization** — underground preserved city, not generic megadungeon.

**Structural note:** After Vol 4, do **not** immediately enter another dungeon. Vol 5 earns emotional investment in community.
