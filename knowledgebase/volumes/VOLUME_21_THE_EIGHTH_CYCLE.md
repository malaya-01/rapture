## Segment beats (planning detail)

### I — Mourning Years (1176–1180)

- Sanctuary grief rituals — Adrian not martyred; remembered as man.
- Cassian continues stewardship without romance rush.
- World political peace fragile: cap accord holds.
- Velkarion memorial — obsidian scale in plaza; children climb it.

### II — Founders' Hands (1181–1186)

- **Rowan:** Retires; garden with Elena; brother's shadow softens.
- **Elena:** School; trains Leon; accepts exhaustion as victory.
- **Marcus:** Archive complete; blindness or tremor optional age beat; pride in Lily.
- **Nora:** Last expedition map; passes compass to junior scout.
- **Viktor:** Old; visits Sanctuary; Cassian shares tea — no friendship, dignity.

### III — Second Generation (1187–1192)

- **Atlas:** First Guardian — Adrian's shoes, own feet.
- **Leon:** Field hospital chief — gentleness as strength.
- **Lily:** Gate engineer — technology serves cap, not conquest.
- Young generation POV: never knew pre-Rapture Earth; Eighth Cycle normal.
- Optional **Selene coda:** learns truth; exile continues; tragic peace.

### IV — Limbo Sunrise (1193–1197)

- Cassian opens limited passage — spatial cost; not permanent portal.
- **Aria sunrise:** decades-long bond closes with image not tragedy.
- Cassian carries Adrian's memory into Limbo visit — Aria honors it.
- Wanderer still incomplete — mystery survives; steward role passed.
- Disciples in Limbo continue — expanded world, not sequel bait.

### V — Last Light (1198–1200)

- Founder's Tree at dusk into dawn — literary symmetry with Ch 1 ordinary morning.
- Cassian alone at end — not lonely; **accompanied by memory and choice**.
- Final line tone: hope without guarantee — Eighth Cycle unwritten.
- No sequel hook — franchise bible supports stories; this arc completes.

## POV & pacing notes

- Epilogue pace: gentle; no new major antagonist.
- Cassian minimum 10 chapters; spread others 2–3 each.
- **Ch 1200** must mirror Ch 1 emotionally — ordinary morning energy in healed world.
- Aria sunrise (1195) = sensory, quiet, earned — not twist.
- Adrian absence felt in every Cassian chapter — not erased by Aria.
- Fade series on image, not speechifying.
- Leave ~10% mystery: why Earth, Azrael full nature, Wanderer completion.

## Series end image (Ch 1200 target)

Cassian at Founder's Tree at dawn. Children's voices. Sky pale gold — not pre-Rapture ordinary, but **whole enough**. He does not look back at the tree carvings. He watches the city wake into the Eighth Cycle.

**End.**
