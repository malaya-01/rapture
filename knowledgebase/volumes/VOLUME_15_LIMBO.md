## Segment beats (planning detail)

### A — Arrival (921–940)

- Sensory wrongness: silence too loud, distance inconsistent, storage affinity misfires.
- Cassian grieves Sanctuary by reconstructing it from memory — memory inaccurate (identity theme).
- Aria introduced: Limbo-native consciousness; curious, lonely, older than she appears.
- No combat loop; threats = existential (forgetting name, losing spatial sense).

### B — The Incomplete (941–960)

- Wanderer = dungeon sovereign of Limbo — **incomplete** by design (see dungeon bible).
- Wanderer does not fight Cassian; watches, tests through conversation and absence.
- Revelation: Limbo holds stewards between cycles — not prison, **waiting room**.
- Cassian anger at being chosen without consent → acceptance through Aria's perspective.

### C — Disciples (961–980)

- Limbo souls aggregate over cycles; Cassian teaches spatial **philosophy** not combat drills.
- Disciples mirror Sanctuary founders — found family recreated in miniature.
- Surface interlude (960): Adrian ages; Nora close to Gate; Marcus decodes Wanderer myth.
- Cassian learns Seventh Gate did not take him — he **walked in** (reframe sacrifice).

### D — Decades (981–990)

- Jump sequences measured in relationship milestones, not power tiers.
- Aria/Cassian: companionship → trust → partnership (fade-to-black intimacy).
- Cassian still loves surface life; Aria knows; no jealousy melodrama.
- Identity climax: Cassian forgets Adrian's eye color briefly — terror of erasure.

### E — Departure Seeds (991–1000)

- Exit condition: anchor must transfer — Cassian cannot hold Gate and live outside.
- Wanderer cannot complete without steward departure — symbiosis not conquest.
- Aria chooses to remain (or fragment remains — decide in draft; either serves arc).
- Cassian commits to return; carries Wanderer's incomplete message.
- Surface: Gate flicker — setup Vol 16–17.

## POV & pacing notes

- Cassian minimum 55 chapters; Aria minimum 12; Wanderer maximum 6.
- Surface interludes: 8–10 chapters total, spaced — never dominate.
- **No boss fight.** Tension from identity, time, love, leaving.
- Disciple chapters = relationship + philosophy; each disciple one defining flaw/gift.
- Aria romance parallel to Adrian arc — not replacement; different realm, different grief.
- Magic limits hold: Cassian cannot portal out at will until exit conditions met.
