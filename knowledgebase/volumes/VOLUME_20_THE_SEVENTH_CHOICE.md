## Segment beats (planning detail)

### Act I — The March (1141–1153)

- Expedition: Cassian, Adrian, Nora, Viktor witness, Velkarion core; Marcus remote link via rune relay.
- Gate interior now navigable — Limbo calibration paid off (character arc, not power).
- Adrian/Cassian quiet intimacy before crisis — fade-to-black if appropriate; emotional not graphic.
- Viktor sees Iron Dominion future depends on outcome — not ally, not enemy.

### Act II — The Anchor (1154–1165)

- Stabilization requires **dual anchors**: spatial (Cassian) + human continuity (Adrian).
- Adrian's death **peaceful**: dies holding Cassian's hand after choosing to release anchor — not stabbed, not monster.
- Velkarion begins sacrifice parallel — dragon biology merges with Gate architecture.
- Nora evacuates teams; Viktor saves Marcus relay when cave-in threatens data.
- Thematic: stewardship costs lives; not because evil fights back — because physics demands it.

### Act III — Broken Cycle (1166–1175)

- Velkarion's body becomes part of failsafe — conscious fragment optional (whisper in wind).
- Gate hum stops worldwide — first silence since Vol 12.
- Cassian returns grieving, victorious, hollow.
- Council declares **Eighth Cycle** possible — not promised utopia.
- Treaty: Viktor signs cap accord; Rowan embraces Cassian without fixing grief.
- Final image: Cassian at Founder's Tree — two names carved.
