## Segment beats (planning detail)

### Act I — The Witnesses (1111–1120)

- Velkarion speaks at length — oldest POV chunk in series; dragon morality.
- Marcus integrates: dungeon bible + Aetherian logs + Limbo disciple records.
- Azrael mentioned as **entropy pressure**, not mastermind — incomplete by design.
- Mother Seraphine's faith challenged publicly — she flees debate (not focus).
- Adrian translates truth for Sanctuary citizens — leadership as translation.

### Act II — Sixth Cycle (1121–1130)

- Sixth Cycle civilization: built World Network hub; dragons partnered; still fell.
- Collapse cause: **exceeded reality's carrying capacity** — mana throughput, not malice.
- Prior stewards entered Limbo; some never left; Wanderer incomplete because seventh cycle interrupted calibration.
- Cassian's 100 years = finishing what sixth steward abandoned.
- Viktor's ideology gains partial validation — restraint matters — but his methods rejected.

### Act III — Seventh Failsafe (1131–1140)

- Seventh Gate **not weapon** — pressure release valve and reset protocol.
- Breaking cycle = new option: capped expansion, distributed stewardship, dragon-human treaty permanent.
- Factions must choose: Iron Dominion fears cap; Free Cities want growth; Sanctuary argues stabilize.
- Velkarion announces he will anchor failsafe at cost of life — foreshadow sacrifice.
- Expedition roster: Cassian, Adrian, Marcus, Nora, Velkarion, Viktor (reluctant witness).
- Romance beat: Cassian/Adrian agree to walk in together — partnership tested by stakes.
