## Segment beats (planning detail)

### Act I — First Winter (881–893)

- Public mourning vs private rage: Elena angry at Cassian; Adrian cannot afford anger.
- Expedition survivors' testimony inconsistent — some swear he stepped into light, others into void.
- Political vultures: Viktor, Seraphine, Free Cities trade consortiums test Sanctuary borders.
- Selene attempts to fill Cassian's social role; Adrian grateful but distant — triggers her fear.
- Founder's Council deadlocks on succession; Adrian refuses title "Founder."

### Act II — Holding Pattern (894–907)

- Year-two montage chapters: seasons, wall repairs, trade routes, minor dungeon clears.
- Adrian as mediator not visionary — contrasts Cassian's spatial genius gap.
- Rowan militarizes; Nora pushes exploration; Marcus buries himself in archives.
- Atlas trains under Rowan; idolizes missing Cassian; tension with Adrian's living presence.
- Gate-adjacent incidents: small teams lost to geometry; Nora documents patterns for later Limbo map.
- Adrian's romance grief: not for Selene — for unspoken friendship with Cassian (still unnamed).

### Act III — The New Normal (908–920)

- Year three: prosperity never fully returns; acceptable stability.
- Sanctuary culture shifts — Founder's Tree becomes shrine and meeting place.
- Selene's integration work praised; jealousy dormant not gone.
- Adrian accepts he may never know how Cassian felt — carries on anyway.
- Final beat: star wrongness reported by child on wall — hook to Vol 15 dual timeline.

## POV & pacing notes

- **No Cassian POV** except one optional dream fragment (Ch 885) — clearly subjective.
- Adrian minimum 14 chapters — this is his grief volume.
- Avoid time-skip montage filler: each season chapter needs one concrete conflict.
- Selene POV minimum 4 chapters — show her choosing good work.
- No new romance; Adrian rejects Selene's romantic overture if attempted (kindly).
- End volume on stillness, not cliffhanger — Limbo volume begins different tone.
