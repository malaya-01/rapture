## Segment beats (planning detail)

### Act I — New Generation (1001–1013)

- Sanctuary demographic shift: born-after-Rapture adults lead departments.
- Atlas: competent, earnest, haunted by Founder's myth; respects Adrian, resents his caution.
- Leon: healer path; humanizes Hale family beyond military.
- Lily + Marcus: mana-tech generation leap.
- Adrian gray at temples; still refuses "Founder" title.
- Political landscape: Viktor strongest since Rapture; Seraphine diminished; Free Cities rich.

### Act II — Selene's Fall (1014–1027)

- **Tragic collapse, not villain reveal:** reader mourns.
- Selene's fear of replaceability conflates Cassian's ghost with Adrian's restraint.
- Exposes her Vol 13 sabotage chain; Adrian feels betrayed on behalf of city, not romance.
- No city-burning climax — she destroys her own legacy (records, trust, integration programs).
- Exile ending default: lives north; redemption door open for epilogue (Vol 21).
- Adrian does not chase her; grief for who she was, not who she became.

### Act III — Viktor's Peak (1028–1040)

- Viktor at height: not invading — **integrating** via treaties Adrian needs.
- Rowan-Viktor respect dialogue — two alphas who believe order saves lives.
- Gate research accelerates: Lily's runes + Nora's maps + Marcus's archives converge.
- Velkarion stirs — single line to Adrian via envoy: "Not dead."
- Final hook: Gate exterior shows constellation from inside Limbo — someone returning.
