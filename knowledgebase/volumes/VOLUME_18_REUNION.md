## Segment beats (planning detail)

### Act I — Unmasking (1081–1090)

- Marcus triggers vault demonstration — keys respond to Cassian in public.
- Cascade: inner circle → Guard → city; rumors faster than truth.
- Rowan military fury; Elena healer fury; Atlas awe; Nora "I knew."
- Adrian avoids Cassian until public address completes — protector instinct inverted.

### Act II — Reckoning (1091–1100)

- Cassian accepts anger without defensiveness — founder as person not icon.
- Limbo summary chapters: decades, disciples, Wanderer — **not training recap**.
- Aria disclosed: Adrian's response defines maturity — asks if she is happy.
- Adrian/Cassian conversations: campfire register from Age 1, older voices.
- Selene mentioned: Cassian learns exile; mourns her; no reunion with Selene here.

### Act III — Together Again (1101–1110)

- Romance: slow, verbal, earned — subtext becomes cautious honesty.
- Co-guardian reinstatement; Cassian refuses mythic Founder seat alone.
- City festival muted — joy and trauma mixed.
- Wanderer message decoded: cycles named — urgent council with Velkarion.
- Final beat: Cassian and Adrian at wall together — first shared future tense.
