# Religions, Beliefs & Mythology

## Design philosophy

No religion entirely correct. No religion entirely wrong. Every faith: truth · misunderstanding · history · myth. **Mystery survives.**

Pre-Rapture faiths **continue** — Christianity, Islam, Hinduism, Buddhism, Judaism, Sikhism, indigenous traditions.

---

## Post-Rapture categories

1. **Continuation faiths** — old religions adapting  
2. **Rapture faiths** — new beliefs after Day Zero  
3. **Ancient faiths** — older than humanity  
4. **Philosophical orders** — ways of life  

---

## FAITH 001 — Keepers of the Last Flame

Rapture faith. Hope must survive. Symbol: Golden Lantern. Strong Sanctuary ally. Inspired by World Relic **The Last Flame**.

## FAITH 002 — Path of the Horizon

Philosophical order. Growth requires movement. Symbol: Silver Compass. Nora aligns. Explorers, scouts, scholars.

## FAITH 003 — Crimson Ascension

Mother Seraphine. Humanity must evolve beyond what it was. Symbol: Crimson Halo. Can drift to extremism.

## FAITH 004 — Memory Keepers

Ancient order. Knowledge is sacred. Aetherians, scholars. Strong academic ally to Sanctuary.

## FAITH 005 — Cycle of Roots

Sylvari, nature communities. Everything returns. Symbol: Tree Root Spiral.

## FAITH 006 — Star Chronicles

Luminari, navigators. Every life is a story. Symbol: Seven-Pointed Star.

---

## Dragon beliefs

No gods. Philosophy of **Legacy** — "What remains after you are gone?" Sacred sites: Dragon Roosts, World Pillars, Celestial Peaks.

---

## Titan myths

Titans as first rulers — gods? creatures? something else? Uncertain.

---

## Creation myths (never confirm which is true)

- Human: humanity chosen  
- Dragon: reality emerged naturally  
- Aetherian: knowledge created existence  
- Luminari: reality is story still being written  

---

## Sanctuary holidays

- **Day of Remembrance** — Rapture dead; candles, memorial walls  
- **Founder's Day** — Sanctuary creation; festivals  
- **First Light Festival** — recovery; lanterns, music  

---

---

## Prophecies

**No chosen-one prophecies.** Use interpretations, warnings, legends — not destiny.

---

## Belief design rule

Every system answers: Where from? Why suffer? What to do? What after death? — **differently per culture**.

**Final philosophy:** Characters find meaning through belief; whether true matters less than what it inspires them to become.
