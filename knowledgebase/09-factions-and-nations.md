# Factions & Nations

## Faction design rule

Every faction: Goals · Strengths · Weaknesses · Culture · Economy · Military · Internal problems

**No faction exists solely to oppose Sanctuary.** Every faction believes it helps humanity survive.

---

## Human civilization tiers

Survivor Camps → Settlements → Cities → City States → Nations → Continental Powers

## FACTION 003 — FREE CITIES

Trade alliance; Golden Compass on Blue Field. Prosperity through cooperation. Close Sanctuary ally. Poor military unity.

## FACTION 004 — SKYBORNE LEAGUE

Explorer confederation; floating islands; aerial superiority; strong Sanctuary ally.

## FACTION 005 — CRIMSON FAITH

Mother Seraphine; Rapture was divine; humanity must transform. Complicated relationship with Sanctuary.

## FACTION 006 — DEEP DELVERS GUILD

Dungeon exploration organization; high casualties; major Sanctuary ally.

## FACTION 007 — GOLDEN EXCHANGE

Merchant consortium; corruption risk; business partners, future rivals.
