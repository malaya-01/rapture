# Creature Companions & Beast Contracts

## Design philosophy

Companions are **characters** — not pets, equipment, or transportation. They have personalities, fears, preferences, growth, relationships.

Readers should cry when a companion dies. Celebrate when they succeed.

**Rule:** No slavery. No forced obedience. No monster collection.

---

## Contract system

Voluntary bond requiring: **Trust · Compatibility · Mutual benefit · Consent**

### Contract types

Companion · Guardian · Combat · Exploration · Soul (extremely rare, dangerous)

### Benefits / risks

Human gains sensory sharing, communication, support. Companion gains mana stabilization, growth, protection.  
If one suffers, both may suffer. Death causes trauma to survivor.

---

## Legendary companions

### Ash — Dire Ashwolf (Atlas Hale)
Black wolf, ember markings, amber eyes. Brave, stubborn, protective. Evolution: Ash Cub → Ashwolf → Dire Ashwolf → Ember Fang → Ash King.

### Whisper — Sky Razor Hawk (Nora Winters)
Silver hawk, moon feathers, golden eyes. Intelligent, mischievous, curious. Scout and messenger.

### Bramble — World Tree Guardian Cub (Lily Vale)
Fox-like, leaf fur, green eyes. Playful, gentle. Future symbol of Sanctuary.

### Tempest — Storm Drake (Adrian Hale)
Blue scales, lightning wings. Proud, fearless, competitive. Argues with Adrian. **Not a true dragon.**

### Lumen — Aether Spirit (Cassian Reed)
**Do not introduce early.** Crystal spirit, silver-blue light. Wise, sarcastic, ancient. Provides perspective, not answers.

### Astralis — Star Dragon (unique, endgame)
Celestial dragon of constellations. Mythic ally.

## Dragon companions

True dragons: **alliance, friendship, partnership, mentorship — never ownership.**

---

## Companion death rule

Deaths **rare**. When they happen, they change people. No disposable animals.
