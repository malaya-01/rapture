# Image Bible

## Generation rules

**Style:** Realistic Epic Fantasy · High Detail · Cinematic Lighting · Book Illustration Quality · Consistent character designs · Consistent architecture · Consistent color language

**Avoid:** Anime · Cartoon · Chibi · Low-detail game assets · Generic AI fantasy

**Audience:** Adult (18+) premium novel illustration — see `23-content-maturity-rating.md`

---

## Content maturity (illustrations)

- **Combat / monsters:** Blood, wounds, and brutal action are allowed when canon-appropriate — not sanitized game-violence
- **Romance:** Tenderness, embraces, kissing — tasteful, never explicit nudity or pornographic poses
- **Horror / hazard:** Ruin, corpses, predation, and environmental dread may be shown honestly
- **Never:** Explicit sexual content, fetish titillation, or shock imagery without story purpose

---

## Visual language

| Context | Colors | Represents |
|---------|--------|------------|
| Sanctuary | White, Silver, Gold, Emerald | Hope, civilization, safety |
| Monster territories | Red, Black, Dark Purple | Danger, chaos, corruption |
| Ancient ruins | Blue, White, Silver | Mystery, lost knowledge |
| Rift zones | Violet, Black, Cosmic Blue | Reality distortion |
